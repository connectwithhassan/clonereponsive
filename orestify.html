Why seek government business Grant opportunities?

Small, medium, and large companies all over the world invest in R&D inputs in order to produce innovative outputs.

Meanwhile, approximately 1/3 of all R&D is funded through public money, e.g., non-dilutive funding sources such as tax credit programs, vouchers, grants, and competitions.

An experienced R&D project proposal writer able to secure public funding of R&D activities can directly and indirectly contribute to the success of your company.

When it comes to R&D project proposal and grant writing, direct benefits stem from the lowered risk and cost of setting up and running an internal R&D team thus improving competitiveness and allowing you to focus on innovating and developing or advancing new products and services.

Indirect benefits relate to spill-over effects, increasing your knowledge base and open innovation network, creating a recruitment talent pool, testing, and identifying new market opportunities while also boosting the performance of R&D teams through diversity and cross-fertilization.

Whether you are a startup looking for funding to develop your MVP, or an established company looking to modernize your offering, there is an R&D scheme out there that can help you achieve your goals by supporting you with the resources you need. For example, public R&D funds could help you hire recent graduates or experienced developers, collaborate with university researchers, buy expensive equipment, or travel and exhibit at prestigious conferences and events.
